Our Story

Our story begins in Colombia, a country rich in natural resources but also facing environmental and social challenges that deeply affect its most vulnerable communities. Growing up in these areas, where hardships are part of everyday life, we have witnessed the struggle for access to clean water and the devastating effects of mercury pollution, especially in communities near artisanal mining operations. These experiences motivated us to seek solutions that create real and positive change.

We met in the classroom, a space where FREDDIE was born, driven by our shared passion for innovation and sustainability. This chance meeting turned into a partnership with a clear purpose: to combine our complementary skills to design a technology that not only protects people and the environment but also ensures a cleaner and more equitable future. FREDDIE is our response to one of the greatest environmental problems in our region, aiming to transform lives and bring hope to those who need it most.
