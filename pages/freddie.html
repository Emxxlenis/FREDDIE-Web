Innovation to Ensure Clean Water and Health in Every Home

A revolutionary device that removes mercury from water, transforming lives in vulnerable communities while promoting sustainability.

How to Use FREDDIE?

FREDDIE is the ideal tool for rural and urban communities that rely on storage tanks or shared water systems. Its installation and use require no advanced technical knowledge, making it accessible to everyone.

Quick and Hassle-Free Installation

FREDDIE is placed directly inside the water storage tank or community reservoir. No adjustments to existing infrastructure or additional equipment are needed.

Automatic and Continuous Purification

Once installed, FREDDIE starts working immediately. As water flows through the system, the device activates its advanced technology to capture and neutralize mercury and other contaminants.

Filtration and Mercury Stabilization Technology

FREDDIE uses a six-layer system combining physical filtration, chemical processes, and electrochemical mechanisms. These layers work together to reduce mercury levels to less than 2%, even under challenging conditions.

Mercury Collection and Reuse

FREDDIE not only purifies water but also safely stores recovered mercury. This mercury can be reused, promoting a circular economy and creating new economic opportunities for the benefiting communities.
